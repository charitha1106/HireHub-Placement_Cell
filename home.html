Academic facilities

IIITM Gwalior offers excellent academic amenities and an ideal learning atmosphere on campus. Our curriculum and courses are designed to be flexible, adapting to new circumstances and ensuring students are always at the forefront of their fields. The programs provide a firm foundation of knowledge while enhancing confidence, creativity, and innovation.

The institute continuously expands its educational programs, incorporating new ideas and advancements into academic activities. Our ultimate purpose is to provide learning opportunities that are relevant and valuable, preparing students for successful careers.

Library

ABV-IIITM's library boasts nearly 30,000 books, extensive journals, magazines, and theses, serving students, faculty, and research scholars. It includes sections for books, theses, e-Learning, a book bank, and a reading room with newspapers and magazines. The library offers extensive online resources and is a member of UGC-INFONET, DELNET, and INDEST AICTE, providing access to over 12,000 online journals. Accessible year-round, it supports a wide range of academic and research needs.

Resources:

Our library is a central hub for learning, featuring a reference library, reading room, and digital library. Equipped with high-speed servers and multimedia computers, it supports extensive research and study activities.

Automation and Access:

The automated system facilitates easy book circulation and journal browsing. A bar-coded identity card system enhances user convenience.

Collections:

The library houses a vast collection of resources, including encyclopedias, dictionaries, handbooks, manuals, journals, and textbooks, essential for students to excel academically and professionally.

Labs

Specialized Labs: Our state-of-the-art laboratories are crucial for hands-on learning and research, ensuring students gain practical experience in their fields. Key labs include:
  • Enterprise Integration Lab
  • Behavioural Economics Experiment and Analytics Lab
  • Applied Chemistry Lab
  • Environmental Science Lab
  • E-business Lab

Additional Facilities

Hostel:

We provide well-appointed single rooms with internet access, good lighting, and ventilation. Amenities include 24/7 filtered drinking water, reading rooms, and recreational facilities.

Sports Facilities:

Our campus features a range of sports facilities, including a swimming pool, gymnasium, lawn tennis court, basketball court, billiards room, squash courts, table tennis boards, and badminton courts, promoting physical fitness and overall well-being.

Conveniences:

To support our students' daily needs, the campus includes a shopping complex, medical facilities, ATM services, a photo studio, and a post office.

Commitment to Excellence

IIITM Gwalior is dedicated to exposing students to global scientific breakthroughs. Students are encouraged to submit research papers and attend international conferences, receiving support to do so. Being in an environment with peers who share similar objectives and ambitions offers an exciting learning experience, fostering success.

Departments and Programs at ABV-IIITM,Gwalior

ABV-IIITM Gwalior is renowned as a leading institute for information technology and management in India. Presently, it provides an opportunity to learn in diverse departments and pursue various academic programs.

This institute has consistently developed over the years to provide students with excellent facilities for holistic development.It hosts departments for management, technical, non-technical and entrepreneurial domains.

B.Tech Programme

Initiated in 2017, this four-year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programme in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) aims to develop high-quality professionals with strong industry relevance.
The programme offers three distinct courses:
- Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science Engineering (BCS)
- Bachelor of Technology in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- Bachelor of Technology in Mathematics & Scientific Computing (MSC)

Key Competencies:
  • Strong computer science fundamentals (Operating System, Computer Networks)
  • Good problem solving skills with strong hold on Data Structures and Algorithms.
  • Development (Android Stack, MERN Stack, Python Stack, JAVA)
  • Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analysis.
  • Blockchain and Web 3.0 development.
  • Database and Cloud Native technologies.

Integrated Post Graduate Programme

This is a 5-year course leading to Dual Degree in Information Technology (IT). Under this programme two distinct courses are offered, namely:
- B.Tech (IT) + M.Tech
- B.Tech (IT) + MBA

Key Competencies:
    B.Tech(IT) + M.Tech(IT)
  • Strong aptitude and analytical skills
  • Full Stack Development (Application, MERN, Python, JAVA, Golang)
  • Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analysis
  • Blockchain and Web 3.0 development
  • Cloud-Native Technologies (Kubernetes, Observability)
    B.Tech(IT) + MBA
  • Full Stack Development (Application, MERN, Python, JAVA, Golang)
  • Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analysis
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Business Analytics, Finance, Operations
  • Strong leadership and managerial skills

Post Graduate M.Tech

This is a 2-year program leading to an M.Tech. degree in the area of:
- Wireless Networks and Computing (WNC)
- Information and Cybersecurity (ICS)- Very Large Scale integration (VLSI) and Embedded Systems.

Key Competencies:
  • System on Chip (SOC)
  • Network on Chip (NOC)
  • Massive MIMO Communication
  • Antenna design for wireless
  • Communication DRA and DRO antenna
  • Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things
  • Multiple access techniques for 5G
  • Infrastructure design, setup, configuration (CN), Redundant Systems (CN)
  • Performance matching (CN)

Post Graduate MBA

The Institute offers a four-semester (2 Years) programme Master of Business Administration (MBA) . The focus of the programme is to integrate IT with business processes. The programme offers various courses falling under the specialized areas of Marketing Management, Technology & Operations Management, IT & Systems, Finance, Human Resource Management, Management of Social Sector, and Business Analytics.

Key Competencies:
  • Marketing, Supply Chain, Human Resources Product Management
  • Ability to leverage technology
  • Interpersonal and Communication Skills Strategic thinking and Planning abilities
  • Time Management and Project Management Skills

Doctoral Program - Ph.D

ABV-IIITM offers doctoral programs in areas of IT, Management and Applied Sciences(Mathematics and Physics). Though there are no time restrictions for Doctoral programs, they are completed within 2-5 years.
Research areas in different disciplines:

  • Engineering:

    Microelectronics/VLSI/Embedded Systems, Computer Architecture, Computer Vision, Algorithms, Image Processing, Robotics, Communication, Networks, Wireless Sensor Networks, IoT, Cyber Physical Systems, Mobile Computing, Grid Computing, Information Security, Soft Computing, Data Mining, Machine Learning, Database/WEB/Multimedia Technologies, Distributed Computing, Analytics, etc.

  • Management:

    Operations, Marketing, Finance, Information Systems Management, Human Resource Management/Organization Behavior, Business Economics, e-Governance, Environment Management, Technology Management, Management of Informal Sector, Project Management, Entrepreneurship, International Business, System Dynamics, Business Analytics, etc.

  • Applied Sciences:

    Physics/ Applied Physics/ Material Science/ Nanotechnology/ Computational Physics/Nanoelectronics/Optoelectronics/Device Modelling/Engineering Physics/Electronics/ Applied Mathematics / Operations Research/ Engineering Mathematics / Reliability / Applied Statistics / Soft Computing Applications / Optimization / Cryptography /Modeling and Simulation / Mathematical Biology, etc.

Departments:

  1. Department of Computer Science and Engineering
  2. Department of Information Technology
  3. Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  4. Department of Management Studies
  5. Department of Engineering Sciences

Why IIITM?

The student body at IIITM Gwalior is exceptionally talented, particularly in the key areas of computer science, IT, and electronics. These students benefit from cutting-edge facilities on campus and are guided by expert faculty members who hold doctorates from prestigious national and international institutions. The faculty's research aligns with current industry and societal demands, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the future. Moreover, the alumni of IIITM Gwalior are a testament to the institution's excellence, having achieved remarkable success in their respective careers. They excel due to their technological expertise, innovative business ideas, and problem-solving skills, meeting the needs of both the public sector and the entrepreneurial world. The Institute takes great pride in its distinguished alumni, who continually bring honor to their alma mater. Additionally, alumni have been instrumental in supporting the Institute, contributing to excellent placement and internship opportunities for current students at prominent international organizations.
